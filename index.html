CV and Competence System

For a consulting company, it's important for your CVs to be up to date and readily available for sale and for skill development. Often CVs are stored in static Word documents and various competency matrices that are difficult to update and keep organized.

Introduce a better experience for your sales team and your employees.

Skills

  • Advanced search and filtering
  • Design the CV template once - Use it for all employees
  • Tailor CVs for different positions
  • Batch download of CVs
  • Dual language support
  • Multiple CV templates

Features

Advanced search and filtering

Quick search engine with filtering options helps you find the right person for the job.

Always updated CVs

No need to rummage a shared folder looking for a document and figuring out which is newest and most updated - With all CV-data stored in a database, you are certain that you get the latest data.

Tailor CVs

Your employees are suitable for different positions - With ACS you can easily tailor your CVs to show the best side to your clients.

CV templates

Always use the latest visual profile for your CV template. Sub-contractor that needs a specific look for the CV? No more headaches having to copy and paste manually to a new CV template.

Ready for Single Sign-On

You don't need to issue new login credentials to every employee for this system - It can be integrated with existing authentication systems.

Dual language

Easily compare and edit two languages in your CVs, make sure your international clients also gets the latest profiles.
